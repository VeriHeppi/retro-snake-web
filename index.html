How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to steer the snake.
  • Eat the glowing food to grow.
  • You can wrap through the edges of the screen.
  • Avoid running into your own body.
  • Press Enter or the Start button to play again.

Controls

  • Arrows / WASD — Move
  • Enter — Start / Restart
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Pause — Pause / Resume
  • Buttons — Toggle music and SFX
